About Me

My name is Gavin Griggs, and I'm a front-end developer in the making, with aspirations of becoming a full-stack developer!
I've been in love with computing ever since my middle school years, where I participated in FIRST®'s Lego® League. I'd then go onto high school learning Lua for Roblox games and even graphic design with Photoshop. Needless to say, my love for creativity meshed well with the design and problem solving that comes with development and software engineering, thus, I ventured deep into Web and App development! As I go deeper into this journey, I'll continue to craft better projects, connect around, and learn more about the field.
